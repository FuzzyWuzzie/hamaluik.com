Brocolli Salad (2020-05-01)

Source: my Mom

Ingredients

Salad

Item Quantity
Broccoli A bowl full
Bacon (Crumbled) 10 strips
Green Onions 4
Raisins ¾ cups
Cheddar Cheese (Grated) ½ cup

Dressing

Item Quantity
Mayonnaise 1 cup
Vinegar (White) 2 teaspoons
Sugar (White) ¼ cup

Instructions

  1. Cut the green onions into bits
  2. Mix
  3. Chill at least 4 hours (or overnight)
